# Billing and subscription

From **Billing and subscription** you can manage plans, payment, usage, and coupons.

## Plans

- **Free**: Get started for free
- **Basic**: For small projects
- **Pro**: For growing businesses
- **Enterprise**: For large organizations (contact sales)

You can choose monthly or annual billing; annual may include a discount.

## Current plan

- Check the plan shown as **Current** and use **Upgrade** to change plan if needed.

## Usage

- View number of services, resource usage, etc.
- Use plan limits and current usage to decide whether to upgrade.

## Coupons

- View your coupons and how to apply them.
- Apply a coupon at checkout to pay a discounted amount.
